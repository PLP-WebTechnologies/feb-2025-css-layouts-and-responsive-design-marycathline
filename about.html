Who We Are

MotherChild Health Organization (MOCHO) is a Kenyan-based, woman-led initiative dedicated to advocating for zero maternal mortality. We provide medical support to less privileged women and children, ensuring they receive complete healthcare at any time.

Our Mission

To empower women and children through accessible and affordable healthcare, education, and sustainable solutions for maternal health.

Our Impact

  • ✅ Supported over 5000 women with maternal healthcare services.
  • ✅ Distributed biodegradable sanitary pads to over 10,000 schoolgirls.
  • ✅ Partnered with Volunteers creative a positive enviroment for women and children.
  • ✅ Conducted financial literacy training for women in Nairobi for 8 years.
